Topic 1: The Water Cycle

Key Concepts & Explanations

The water cycle is the continuous movement of water on, above, and below the surface of the Earth. Key processes include:

  • Evaporation: Water changes from liquid to gas
  • Condensation: Water vapor cools and forms clouds
  • Precipitation: Water falls as rain, snow, etc.
  • Collection: Water returns to oceans, lakes, etc.
Quiz Questions (10)
  1. What is the process called when water changes from a liquid to a gas?
  2. Which part of the water cycle returns water to Earth's surface?
  3. What drives the water cycle on Earth?
