📅Agreement Terms & Duration

Contract Period: This agreement begins on January 15, 2025, and continues for an initial term of 24 months, ending on January 14, 2027.

Renewal: Agreement automatically renews for successive 12-month periods unless either party provides 60 days written notice of non-renewal.

Termination Conditions:

  • Either party may terminate with 60 days written notice
  • Immediate termination for material breach after 30-day cure period
  • Termination for convenience requires 90 days notice and payment of outstanding fees
  • Upon termination, all tenant security deposits transfer to owner
  • Property Manager entitled to management fees through termination date
Early Termination Fee: If owner terminates without cause within first 12 months, owner pays early termination fee equal to 2 months management fees.

⚖️Legal Terms & Conditions

Governing Law: This agreement is governed by Illinois state law and local Springfield ordinances.

Dispute Resolution: Any disputes will be resolved through binding arbitration in Springfield, Illinois.

Indemnification: Each party agrees to indemnify the other for damages arising from their negligent acts or omissions.

Insurance Requirements:

  • Owner maintains property insurance with Manager as additional insured
  • Manager maintains professional liability insurance ($1M minimum)
  • Manager maintains general liability insurance ($2M minimum)
  • Both parties maintain errors & omissions coverage

Confidentiality: Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of financial information, tenant data, and proprietary business practices.
