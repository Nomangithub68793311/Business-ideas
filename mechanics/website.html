Auto Tips & FAQs

How Often Should You Change Your Oil?

Most modern vehicles require oil changes every 5,000-7,500 miles, but check your owner's manual for specific recommendations. Factors like driving conditions and oil type affect frequency.

Published: January 15, 2025

Signs Your Brakes Need Attention

Warning signs include squealing sounds, grinding noises, vibration when braking, or a soft brake pedal. Don't ignore these symptoms - brake safety is crucial.

Published: January 10, 2025

Winter Car Care Tips

Prepare your vehicle for winter with proper tire pressure, antifreeze levels, battery checks, and emergency kit preparation. Prevention is key to winter reliability.

Published: December 20, 2024

When to Replace Your Car Battery

Most car batteries last 3-5 years. Signs of a failing battery include slow engine cranking, dim headlights, and dashboard warning lights. Test annually after 3 years.

Published: December 15, 2024

FAQ: Check Engine Light

Q: What does the check engine light mean?
A: It indicates an engine or emissions system problem. While you can usually drive briefly, get it diagnosed promptly to prevent damage.

Published: December 1, 2024

FAQ: Transmission Maintenance

Q: How often should I service my transmission?
A: Automatic transmissions typically need service every 30,000-60,000 miles. Manual transmissions may go longer but check fluid levels regularly.
